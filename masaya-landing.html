[ 01 ] About

A practice
shaped by
three continents.

Masaya has lived across three countries and worked alongside people from every corner of the world. From Tokyo to Chicago, a decade of leading and contributing to unprecedented experience design, strategy, and marketing work has produced a way of thinking that integrates diverse perspectives and disciplines into single, coherent moves.

The work brings cultural awareness, a sensitivity to small differences, and the high motivation to keep endeavoring — the qualities organizations need when the brief is ambiguous and the ecosystem is the actual problem.

"Just as an admirable chef is usually exposed to the best and unique ingredients and cuisine, it takes a unique and creative way of life to create creative innovations."
